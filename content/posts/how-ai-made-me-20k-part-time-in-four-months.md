> You are scared of AI replacing you while you should be scared of someone using AI replacing you.

"With a 3.7GPA and a concentration in Machine Learning from my grad school, the world is at my feet" thought the naïve me. Thousands of international students come to the US every year with a similar dream. But the advent of AI changed everything for students coming fresh out of college - no one wants to hire someone with no experience when AI can do the same job for them without the learning curve and fraction of the cost.

This was especially devastating for students on a visa like me because the clock starts ticking and I had to find a job within that small window. I don't mind going back home but I have a student loan which is quicker to pay off if I earn in USD. This created a painful and super stressful couple of months where I was doing all I could to get a job - going to career fairs, reaching out to people on LinkedIn, cold emailing recruiters, and attending free conferences and meetups.

An opportunity to speak at a conference came up and I grabbed it. This is where I met the guy who would propose this $20k job to me.
