In 2015, one of the biggest problems that needed solving was [machine translation](https://www.ibm.com/think/topics/machine-translation). A huge transition was taking place to using neural sequence-to-sequence models based machine translation approaches rather than the statistical phrase tables based approach which was predominant at the time. One of the major breakthroughs was the introduction of the [attention mechanism]() by Bahdanau et al.
